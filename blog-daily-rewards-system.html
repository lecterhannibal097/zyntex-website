Introducing the Daily Rewards System

November 2024 4 min read Features

Mining ZYM tokens is the core of the Zyntex experience, but it is far from the only way to earn. We have built a comprehensive daily rewards system designed to reward active, engaged community members with bonus ZYM on top of their regular mining earnings. Whether you have two minutes or ten minutes to spare each day, there are rewards waiting for you.

This article walks through every component of the rewards system, explains how each one works, and shows you how to maximize your daily earnings without spending a single dollar.

The Three Pillars of Daily Rewards

The Zyntex rewards system is organized into three tabs within the Rewards section of the app. Each tab represents a distinct category of earning opportunity, and each has its own mechanics and reset schedule.

1. Daily Tasks

Daily tasks are a rotating set of simple activities that refresh every 24 hours at midnight UTC. Each completed task awards a fixed amount of bonus ZYM directly to your balance. These rewards are separate from and in addition to whatever you earn through mining.

The daily task list typically includes the following types of activities:

  • Daily check-in: Simply opening the app and claiming your daily login reward. This is the easiest ZYM you will ever earn. Tap, claim, done.
  • Start a mining session: Activating mining for the day. Since you should be doing this anyway to maintain your streak, this task rewards you extra for a habit you are already building.
  • Visit the rewards page: Navigating to the Rewards section. This ensures you are aware of all available earning opportunities each day.
  • Complete a social action: Tasks like sharing content or engaging with the community in a specific way.

The number and type of daily tasks may vary as we add new activities and adjust the system based on user feedback. New task types will be announced through in-app notifications and our social channels.

Completion bonus: Completing all available daily tasks in a single day triggers an additional completion bonus. This bonus is a multiplier applied to the total daily task rewards, making it significantly more valuable to finish every task rather than just a few.

2. Follow & Earn (Social Tasks)

The Follow and Earn tab contains one-time tasks that reward you for following Zyntex on various social media platforms. Unlike daily tasks, these do not reset. Once you complete a social follow task, you receive the reward permanently and the task is marked as done.

Available social follow tasks include:

  • Follow on Telegram: Join the official Zyntex Telegram community
  • Follow on Twitter/X: Follow the Zyntex Twitter account for updates
  • Follow on Instagram: Follow the Zyntex Instagram profile
  • Join Discord: Join the Zyntex Discord server
  • Subscribe on YouTube: Subscribe to the Zyntex YouTube channel

Each social platform awards a specific amount of ZYM upon verification. The rewards are generous because growing our social media presence helps the entire community by increasing Zyntex's visibility and attracting new miners.

How Verification Works

When you tap a social follow task, you are directed to the relevant platform to complete the follow action. After completing it, return to the Zyntex app and tap the verify button. The system confirms your action and credits the ZYM to your balance. In most cases, verification is instant.

One-time but valuable: If you have not yet completed all social follow tasks, you are sitting on unclaimed ZYM. Take five minutes to work through the entire list. It is the highest ZYM-per-minute activity available in the app.

3. Watch & Earn (Ad Rewards)

The Watch and Earn tab allows you to earn bonus ZYM by watching short video advertisements. Each ad typically lasts between fifteen and thirty seconds, and you earn a fixed ZYM reward for each one you watch.

Daily Ad Limits

There is a daily limit on how many ads you can watch, which resets at midnight UTC along with the daily tasks. The limit exists to balance reward distribution across the user base. Make sure you use all available ad views each day, as unused views do not carry over to the next day.

Ad Availability

The number of available ads at any given time depends on advertiser demand in your region. If you tap Watch and Earn and no ads are available, try again later in the day. Advertiser campaigns rotate throughout the day, and new inventory frequently becomes available. Peak availability tends to be during daytime hours in major advertising markets.

We are continuously working to expand our advertising partnerships to ensure consistent ad availability across all regions where Zyntex is active.

Streak Bonuses: The Consistency Multiplier

Beyond the three reward tabs, the daily rewards system includes a streak mechanic that rewards consistent daily engagement. Your streak counter increments each consecutive day that you complete at least one mining session and claim at least one daily task.

How Streaks Build

Every day you meet the minimum engagement threshold (mine + claim), your streak counter goes up by one. Miss a single day and the counter resets to zero. The longer your streak, the higher the bonus multiplier applied to certain rewards.

  • Week 1 (Days 1-7): Building phase, no bonus yet
  • Week 2 (Days 8-14): First streak tier unlocked with a modest bonus
  • Week 3-4 (Days 15-30): Enhanced bonus tier with noticeably higher rewards
  • Month 2+ (Days 31+): Maximum streak tier with the highest multiplier available

The streak bonus applies to your base mining rate and certain daily task rewards. Over the course of weeks and months, the cumulative effect of a maintained streak is substantial. A miner at the maximum streak tier earns meaningfully more than a miner at the same base rate with no streak.

Streak protection tip: Life happens and sometimes you might forget to open the app. Set a daily reminder on your phone for a consistent time each day. Many of our most successful miners set an alarm for their morning routine. The entire daily process takes less than two minutes.

Reward Calculation: How It All Adds Up

To illustrate how the different reward components stack together, here is a breakdown of what a fully optimized day looks like for an active Zyntex miner:

  1. Base mining earnings: Your hourly mining rate multiplied by the hours in your session
  2. Streak multiplier: Applied on top of your base mining earnings
  3. Referral bonus: Percentage boost from active referrals in your network
  4. Daily task rewards: Flat ZYM bonuses for completing each task
  5. Completion bonus: Extra multiplier for finishing all daily tasks
  6. Ad rewards: Flat ZYM bonuses for each ad watched

Each component is additive and independent. Mining earnings plus streak multiplier plus referral bonus form your session earnings. Daily task rewards, completion bonus, and ad rewards are added on top as flat bonuses. The total daily earnings of an optimized miner can be several times higher than a miner who only taps the mine button and does nothing else.

Upcoming Reward Features

The daily rewards system is actively being expanded. Here is a preview of features we are working on for future releases:

  • Weekly challenges: Multi-day tasks that offer larger rewards for sustained effort
  • Achievement badges: Permanent profile badges earned by reaching specific milestones
  • Seasonal events: Limited-time reward campaigns with exclusive bonuses and multipliers
  • Community goals: Collective milestones where the entire community works toward a shared reward

These features will be announced through our blog and social channels as they become available. Join our Telegram and Discord communities to be the first to know about new earning opportunities.

Your Daily Rewards Checklist

To make sure you never miss a reward, here is a simple daily checklist you can follow:

  1. Open Zyntex and start your mining session
  2. Go to the Daily Tasks tab and complete every available task
  3. Check the Follow and Earn tab for any uncompleted social tasks
  4. Visit the Watch and Earn tab and watch all available ads
  5. Verify that your streak counter has incremented

Total time required: approximately two to five minutes. Total impact: significantly higher ZYM accumulation over weeks and months compared to mining alone.

The rewards system exists because we want to reward the community members who are most engaged and committed to the Zyntex ecosystem. Every task you complete, every ad you watch, and every day you maintain your streak strengthens both your personal ZYM balance and the platform as a whole.

Start building your streak today. Your future self will appreciate the consistency.
