How the Referral System Works

Every Zyntex user gets a unique referral code after signing up. This code can be shared with anyone. When a new user signs up using your code, they become part of your team — and their active mining contributes to your rate boost.

Here's the key mechanic: your team mining boost is determined by the number of active miners on your team, not just the total number of sign-ups. This means quality matters more than quantity — an engaged miner on your team is worth far more than someone who signs up and never opens the app.

The Team Mining Boost Explained

Each active member of your team contributes a percentage boost to your base mining rate. The more active miners you have, the higher your total rate. This creates a snowball effect: the bigger your team, the faster you mine, the more ZYM you accumulate.

📊 Example: If you have 10 active team members each contributing a 25% boost, your mining rate could be significantly higher than a solo miner with no team. Over a 30-day period, this difference in earnings can be substantial.

The team boost stacks with other boosts like the Security Circle bonus, creating a compounding multiplier effect on your earnings.

Where to Share Your Referral Code

WhatsApp & Telegram Groups

Messaging app groups are among the most effective channels for referral sharing. Post in relevant groups — crypto enthusiasts, tech communities, students, or any group where people are open to earning opportunities. A personalized message explaining what Zyntex is converts much better than just dropping a link.

Social Media

Share on Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, and TikTok. Short-form video content explaining "how I earn crypto from my phone" tends to perform well organically. Include your referral code in your bio or post description.

YouTube & Blogs

If you create content, making a video or writing a post about Zyntex and your mining experience is a powerful long-term referral strategy. Content that ranks in search can generate referrals passively for months.

Friends and Family

Don't underestimate word-of-mouth. Personal recommendations from trusted contacts have the highest conversion rates. Explain the app to friends and family directly — they're more likely to stay active, which maximizes your ongoing rate boost.

Tips for Keeping Your Team Active

Getting referral sign-ups is only half the battle. The real value comes from keeping your team engaged. Here are proven strategies:

  • Create a group chat: Start a WhatsApp or Telegram group for your Zyntex team. Sharing mining tips and encouraging each other dramatically improves retention.
  • Share milestones: Post your ZYM balance achievements to inspire your team to stay active.
  • Explain the benefits: Make sure your referrals understand why daily mining matters — many people drop off simply because they don't understand the value of consistency.
  • Lead by example: If you're visibly active and earning, your team will follow your lead.
