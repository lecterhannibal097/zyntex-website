KYC Verification on Zyntex: Why It Matters and How to Complete It

March 19, 20256 min readGuides
By the Zyntex Compliance Team

Know Your Customer (KYC) verification is one of those aspects of crypto that users often put off until they absolutely need it. On Zyntex, KYC is required before you can withdraw your mined ZYM tokens. The smart move is to complete it now, while your balance is building, so you are not scrambling to finish the process the day exchange listings go live.

This guide explains exactly what KYC is, why it is a legal requirement rather than an optional extra, what documents you need, how to complete the process step by step, and what to do if your verification is rejected.

What Is KYC and Why Is It Required?

KYC, or Know Your Customer, is the process by which financial platforms verify the identity of their users. It is a legal requirement in most jurisdictions for platforms that handle the transfer of financial value — which includes cryptocurrency withdrawal and exchange features.

The regulations behind KYC exist to prevent money laundering, terrorist financing, fraud, and tax evasion. By verifying that each account belongs to a real, identified person, platforms create an audit trail that makes it significantly harder to use cryptocurrency for illegal purposes.

We understand that some users are skeptical of providing personal information to crypto platforms — there have been high-profile data breaches in the industry. This is why Zyntex uses a dedicated, industry-standard identity verification provider with strict data handling protocols, rather than storing your ID documents directly on our servers.

Key point: KYC is not something Zyntex chose to impose — it is a legal obligation for any platform enabling cryptocurrency withdrawals. Platforms that skip KYC face regulatory shutdown. Your identity documents are processed by a professional verification provider, not stored by Zyntex directly.

What Documents You Need

Zyntex accepts government-issued identity documents from over 120 countries. You will need one of the following:

  • National identity card — front and back
  • Passport — photo page
  • Driver's license — front and back

The document must be current and not expired. It must clearly show your full name, date of birth, document number, and photo. The photo you submit for the selfie step must clearly match the face on the document.

Ensure your documents are in good condition before submitting. Blurry photos, partially visible documents, or documents with glare are the most common reasons for initial verification rejections.

KYC Levels on Zyntex

Zyntex uses a tiered KYC system with different verification levels unlocking different platform capabilities:

  • Level 0 (No KYC): Mining, earning, and building your balance. No withdrawals.
  • Level 1 (Basic KYC): Email and phone verified. Access to basic platform features.
  • Level 2 (Full KYC): Government ID verified. Required for ZYM withdrawals and wallet release.
  • Level 3 (Enhanced KYC): Additional verification for large withdrawal amounts above certain thresholds.

Most users need to complete Level 2 to access full withdrawal capabilities. Level 3 is only required if your account holds a very large ZYM balance and you want to withdraw it all at once.

How to Complete KYC Verification: Step by Step

1

Open the KYC Section

In the Zyntex app, go to Profile → KYC Verification. You will see your current verification level and a button to begin the verification process.

2

Select Your Document Type and Country

Choose your country and the type of identity document you are using. The system will show you exactly which photos are required for your selected document type.

3

Take or Upload Document Photos

Photograph your document in good lighting on a flat, dark surface. Make sure all corners are visible, the text is readable, and there is no glare obscuring any part of the document. For ID cards and driver's licenses, front and back photos are both required.

4

Take a Selfie

Take a clear selfie showing your face in good lighting. Hold the selfie naturally — do not wear sunglasses, hats, or face coverings. The verification system will compare your face to the photo on your document automatically.

5

Review and Submit

Review all your submitted photos before confirming. Once submitted, verification typically completes within a few minutes for automatic approvals, or up to 24 hours for manual review cases.

Common Reasons for KYC Rejection and How to Fix Them

Blurry or Low-Quality Photos

This is the most common rejection reason. Retake your document photos in better lighting, holding the phone completely still, and ensure the entire document is in frame with no parts cut off. Avoid photographing documents on shiny surfaces that cause glare.

Expired Document

Check the expiry date on your document before submitting. An expired document will always be rejected. If your primary ID has expired, use a different document type that is current.

Name Mismatch

The name on your identity document must match the name on your Zyntex account. If you registered with a nickname or abbreviated name, update your profile name before submitting KYC.

Selfie Does Not Match Document

Ensure your selfie is taken in the same session as your document upload. Use good lighting and look directly at the camera. If you have significantly changed your appearance since your document photo was taken (major haircut, beard, etc.), the manual review process will typically still approve you.

Privacy and Data Security

We take the privacy of your identity documents seriously. Your submitted documents are processed by an industry-standard verification provider operating under strict data protection regulations. Zyntex does not retain copies of your raw identity documents on our servers after verification is complete — only the verification result and a reference ID are stored in your account profile.

If you have questions about how your data is handled during KYC, our privacy policy and dedicated KYC data handling document are available on the website.

Our recommendation: Complete your KYC verification now, while the process is straightforward and unhurried. Users who wait until exchange listings are announced will face high demand on the verification system and potential delays. Early KYC completion means you can withdraw the moment trading opens.
