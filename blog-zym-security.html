How We Keep Your ZYM Balance Safe

📅 January 2025⏱ 4 min read✍️ Zyntex Team
← Back to Blog

Security is not an afterthought at Zyntex — it's a foundational principle. When you mine ZYM tokens, your balance represents real value that you've earned through time and effort. We take that responsibility seriously. Here's an in-depth look at the security systems we've built to protect your account and your earnings.

Account Security

📧

Email Verification

Every new account must verify their email address before accessing the app. Unverified accounts cannot mine or earn rewards, preventing bot accounts from flooding the network.

🔢

OTP Authentication

Every login requires a one-time password sent to your registered email. This means even if someone knows your password, they cannot access your account without also controlling your email.

🔑

Private Key Backup

Users can generate and securely back up a private key for their account. This gives you an additional recovery method that's entirely under your control.

📍

Login Activity Monitoring

Your profile shows a full history of login activity including device type, location, and timestamp. You can review this anytime and flag suspicious access.

Balance Protection

Your ZYM balance is stored in Firebase Firestore — Google's enterprise-grade cloud database. But storage alone isn't enough. We've implemented hardened security rules that protect your balance at the database level:

  • Balance can only increase through legitimate mining — direct manipulation attempts are rejected at the database rule level.
  • Mining rate caps are enforced server-side — no client can claim an impossibly high mining rate.
  • Transaction records are immutable once created — every ZYM movement is permanently logged.
  • Withdrawal requests are reviewed before processing — no automatic outflows without verification.

🛡️ Important: Zyntex will never ask for your existing cryptocurrency wallet's private keys. If anyone claiming to be Zyntex asks for this, it is a scam. Your ZYM balance exists entirely within the Zyntex system until you choose to release it through our official process.

KYC Verification

Before any ZYM can be released to an external wallet, users must complete our Know Your Customer (KYC) verification process. KYC serves multiple purposes:

  • Prevents one person from controlling multiple accounts
  • Meets regulatory requirements for financial services
  • Protects the network from fraudulent activity
  • Ensures ZYM tokens are released to verified, legitimate owners

KYC is a standard requirement across legitimate cryptocurrency platforms. It protects both individual users and the integrity of the entire network.

Anti-Fraud Systems

We have multiple systems in place to detect and prevent fraudulent activity on the Zyntex network:

  • Automated detection of suspicious mining patterns
  • Rate limiting on all sensitive operations
  • Firestore security rules that validate every write operation
  • Admin monitoring tools for flagging anomalous accounts

What You Can Do to Stay Secure

  • Use a strong, unique password for your email account.
  • Never share your OTP code with anyone — Zyntex staff will never ask for it.
  • Back up your private key and store it in a safe place.
  • Regularly check your login activity for any unrecognized sessions.
  • Only download Zyntex from the official Google Play Store listing.

Your security is a shared responsibility. We provide the infrastructure; you protect your credentials. Together, we keep the Zyntex network safe for everyone.
