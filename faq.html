Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Zyntex, ZYM tokens, and mobile mining.

⛏️ Getting Started

What is Zyntex? ▼
Zyntex is a mobile crypto mining platform that allows anyone with a smartphone to earn ZYM tokens. Unlike traditional cryptocurrency mining, Zyntex requires no expensive hardware or technical knowledge. You simply download the app, create an account, and start mining.
Is Zyntex free to use? ▼
Yes, Zyntex is completely free to download and use. There are no fees to start mining. You earn ZYM tokens for free through your daily mining sessions, tasks, and referrals. Optional features like watching reward ads are voluntary and always free.
What devices does Zyntex support? ▼
Zyntex is currently available on Android devices via the Google Play Store. An iOS version is planned for a future release. Your device needs to run Android 7.0 or higher to install and run the app.
How do I create an account? ▼
Download Zyntex from the Google Play Store, open the app, and tap "Sign Up." Enter your email address and create a password. You'll receive a verification email — click the link to verify your account, then you can start mining immediately.

💰 Mining & ZYM Tokens

How does mining work in Zyntex? ▼
Mining in Zyntex works by activating a mining session in the app. Once active, you earn ZYM tokens at your current mining rate for up to 24 hours. You need to return to the app at least once per day to confirm your session and keep it running. Sessions that aren't confirmed pause automatically.
What is the ZYM mining rate? ▼
The base mining rate in the Genesis phase is 24 ZYM per 24-hour session. Your actual rate can be higher depending on your Security Circle bonus, team referral boost, and other multipliers. The rate is applied continuously during your active session.
What is the halving system? ▼
Zyntex operates on an 8-phase network model. As the network grows and reaches user milestones, the mining rate multiplier decreases (halves). This is designed to reward early adopters — the earlier you mine, the higher your rate. Once a phase transition occurs, the rate does not go back up.
What is ZYM worth? ▼
ZYM is currently in its pre-exchange phase. Its market value will be determined when it is listed on cryptocurrency exchanges, which is a planned milestone on our roadmap. In the meantime, accumulating ZYM at today's high mining rates positions you well for when exchange listing occurs.
Can I lose my ZYM balance? ▼
Your ZYM balance is stored securely in our database and protected by multiple security layers. Balances can only decrease through voluntary withdrawals you initiate. We do not and cannot deduct from your balance for inactivity or any other reason.
👥 Referrals & Security Circle
How do referrals boost my mining rate? ▼
Every active miner you refer to Zyntex joins your team and contributes a percentage boost to your mining rate. The more active miners on your team, the higher your total mining rate. Only actively mining team members contribute to your boost.
What is the Security Circle? ▼
The Security Circle is a trust network of up to 5 people you personally know. Each member you add to your circle gives you a +5% mining rate boost. A full circle of 5 members provides a +25% permanent bonus to your mining rate.
Is there a limit to how many people I can refer? ▼
There is no limit on referrals. You can refer as many people as you like. Each active referral contributes to your team mining boost. The Security Circle, however, is limited to 5 members by design.

🏦 Withdrawals & KYC

When can I withdraw my ZYM? ▼
ZYM withdrawals to external wallets require KYC (Know Your Customer) verification. KYC is currently being rolled out. Once your KYC is approved, you can release your mined balance to a connected external wallet.
What is KYC and why is it required? ▼
KYC (Know Your Customer) is an identity verification process required by financial regulations. It prevents fraud, ensures one person cannot control multiple accounts, and is a standard requirement for cryptocurrency platforms that allow asset withdrawals. KYC is required only when you want to release ZYM to an external wallet.
Is there a fee for withdrawals? ▼
A small release fee applies when converting your mined ZYM balance to wallet balance. This fee covers transaction processing costs. The fee percentage is displayed clearly before you confirm any release.

🔒 Security & Account

What should I do if I forget my password? ▼
Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen. You'll receive a password reset email. If you're having trouble, contact our support team at support@zyntexnetwork.com and we'll help you regain access.
Will Zyntex ever ask for my cryptocurrency wallet's private key? ▼
No. Zyntex will never ask for your external cryptocurrency wallet's private key. Your ZYM balance exists within the Zyntex system. If anyone claiming to be Zyntex asks for your private key, it is a scam — please report it to support@zyntexnetwork.com immediately.
How do I delete my account? ▼
You can request account deletion by contacting support@zyntexnetwork.com. Please note that account deletion is irreversible and any unmined ZYM balance will be forfeited. We recommend withdrawing your balance before requesting deletion.
