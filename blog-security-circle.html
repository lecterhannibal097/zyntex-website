Introducing Security Circle: Build Your Trust Network

📅 May 2025⏱ 4 min read✍️ Zyntex Team
← Back to Blog

One of the most exciting features to arrive in Zyntex version 1.3.5 is the Security Circle — a trust-based network mechanic that rewards you for building genuine relationships within the Zyntex community. In this post, we'll explain what the Security Circle is, how it works, and why it's one of the best ways to permanently boost your mining rate.

What Is the Security Circle?

The Security Circle is a group of up to 5 people you personally trust and invite into your inner network. It's inspired by the concept used in Pi Network, where a trusted inner circle helps validate user identity and strengthen the community fabric.

In Zyntex, each member of your Security Circle adds a direct +5% boost to your mining rate. A full circle of 5 members gives you a permanent +25% bonus on top of your base rate — one of the highest single boosts available in the app.

How to Build Your Security Circle

  1. Open the Zyntex app and go to the Home screen.
  2. Tap the Security Circle card.
  3. Tap Add Member and search for a user by their username.
  4. Send them an invite — they'll receive a notification to accept or decline.
  5. Once accepted, their +5% boost is added to your mining rate immediately.
  6. Repeat until all 5 slots are filled.

🔒 Important: Only add people you genuinely know and trust. Your Security Circle represents your real-world connections within the Zyntex network.

Who Should You Add?

The best Security Circle members are people who are actively mining every day. Since their active participation contributes to your boost, you want members who are engaged with the platform. Here are the best candidates:

  • Friends and family who are already using Zyntex
  • Active referrals you've brought into the platform
  • Trusted members of your Zyntex community group
  • Colleagues or classmates who mine regularly

Security Circle vs. Team Referrals

Both the Security Circle and referral team contribute to your mining rate, but they work differently. Your referral team can grow to any size — there's no limit to how many people you can refer. The Security Circle, however, is limited to 5 members and is designed for your closest, most trusted connections.

The two systems stack together, meaning a miner with a full Security Circle AND a large active referral team earns at a significantly higher rate than someone relying on just one of these mechanisms.

What Happens If a Circle Member Stops Mining?

If a member of your Security Circle becomes inactive, their contribution to your boost may be reduced. This is designed to keep the circle healthy and active. If a member goes inactive for an extended period, you can remove them and invite someone more engaged in their place.

This is why it's important to maintain your circle actively — reach out to your members, encourage them to stay engaged, and replace anyone who has stopped using the app.

Start Building Your Circle Today

The Security Circle is available right now in the latest version of the Zyntex app. Update to version 1.3.5 or later, open the app, and start inviting your trusted contacts. With rates at their highest during the Genesis phase, every percentage of mining boost matters.
