Login Streak Rewards

Your login streak is one of the most valuable long-term assets in Zyntex. The longer you maintain it, the bigger your bonus rewards:

How Much Can You Earn From Rewards?

Let's look at what a fully engaged Zyntex user earns per day from the rewards system alone, not counting their base mining rate:

  • Daily check-in: +5 ZYM
  • 5 reward ads: +10 ZYM
  • Streak bonus (30-day): +25 ZYM (once per milestone)

On a regular day without a streak milestone, a fully engaged user earns 15+ ZYM in bonus rewards on top of their mining income. Over a month, that's 450+ ZYM from rewards alone — nearly as much as base mining earnings.

Tips to Maximize Reward Earnings

  • Complete all daily tasks first thing in the morning — it takes less than 5 minutes and sets your earnings for the day.
  • Never break your streak — even on busy days, a 10-second app open is all it takes.
  • Claim all social follow rewards immediately — they're one-time bonuses that many users forget about.
  • Watch all 5 ads every day — the progressive reward model means the last ad is worth more than the first.

The Daily Rewards System is designed to reward the most engaged Zyntex users. Combined with active mining, a strong referral team, and a full Security Circle, daily tasks can push your total ZYM earnings to levels far beyond what passive miners achieve. Start completing your daily tasks today.
